# Super Mo - Sprite Sheet Production Plan

## Canvas
- Base tile: 16x16
- Character scale: 16x24 (player), 16x16 (enemies)

## Player (Super Mo)
- Idle: 4 frames
- Run: 6 frames
- Jump: 1 frame
- Fall: 1 frame
- Stomp: 2 frames
- Hurt: 1 frame

## Enemies
- Moomba: 2 frames
- Spikelet: 2 frames
- Flit: 2 frames

## Tiles
- Ground: top/mid/edge/corner (8 tiles)
- Platform: 4 tiles
- Question block: 4 frames
- Solid block: 1 tile
- One-way platform (optional): 1 tile

## Collectibles
- Coin: 4 frames
- Star Shard: 6 frames

## Effects
- Stomp puff: 3 frames
- Coin sparkle: 3 frames

## Export
- PNG sprite sheet + JSON atlas
- Keep 2px padding between sprites
