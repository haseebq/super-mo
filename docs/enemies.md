# Super Mo - Enemy Roster

## Enemy 1: Moomba (Walker)
- **Behavior:** Walks back and forth, turns at edges or walls.
- **Defeat:** Stomp from above.
- **Purpose:** Intro enemy for timing and stomp.

## Enemy 2: Spikelet (Spiky)
- **Behavior:** Slow walker with spikes; cannot be stomped.
- **Defeat:** Avoid or hit from side with optional power (Phase 2).
- **Purpose:** Teaches avoidance and jump timing.

## Enemy 3: Flit (Flying)
- **Behavior:** Patrols a short vertical path.
- **Defeat:** Stomp from above or avoid.
- **Purpose:** Adds vertical timing challenge.

## Common Rules
- Enemies pause 150ms when hit.
- Player bounce on successful stomp.
- Visual cues: color and shape distinct per enemy.
