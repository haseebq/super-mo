# Story Arc

## Premise
The valley is losing its light. Ancient shards scattered across the land can rekindle the sky, but only if the runner reaches each gate before the glow fades.

## Main Beats
1. **Level 1: The Wake-Up Run**
   - The Guide introduces the mission and basic movement.
   - The player learns to reach the goal and collect early shards.
2. **Level 2: Wind Carvings**
   - Platforms and gaps widen; the runner chases swirling light.
   - The first checkpoint is introduced as a safe anchor.
3. **Level 3: Broken Bridges**
   - The valley fractures; the runner must use speed boosts to leap through splits.
   - The Guide warns of the storm rising.
4. **Level 4: River of Mist**
   - Moving platforms and waterfalls test timing.
   - Shards hint at a hidden path with a reward.
5. **Level 5: Skyline Rush**
   - Vertical climbs and quick hazards demand confident runs.
   - The runner gathers the last shards for the final gate.
6. **Level 6: The Gate of Dawn**
   - The final stretch to restore the sky.
   - Reaching the goal restores light to the valley.

## Characters
- **The Runner**: Silent hero who carries the valley's hope.
- **The Guide**: A calm voice guiding the journey and revealing stakes.

## Tone
Warm, hopeful, and urgent. The narrative should feel like a gentle push to keep moving.

## Onboarding Story Intro (Level 1)
- "Welcome, runner. The valley is waking up."
- "Reach the goal flag and gather coins and shards."
- "Use your jump and run boosts to cross the gaps."
- "Each checkpoint will hold your progress."
